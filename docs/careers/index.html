Overview

Hadron is building the first public, decentralized cloud computing platform. A new kind of world computer with transformative global, economic, and social impact, we are democratizing access to a performant, tamperproof, infinitely scalable, distributed computing system. Hadron is backed by top-tier institutions including Polychain Capital and Andreessen Horowitz. You will work alongside a world-class technical & research team who value out-of-the-box thinking and embrace a highly collaborative environment. Hadron is supportive of our engineers and researchers being active and speaking in their technical communities globally.

What We Offer

Competitive Remuneration

We are offering a highly competitive performance-based remuneration package, recognizing commitment and target achievements.

Trainings

We invest an average of 40 hours of extracurricular education annually, offer different forms of trainings and knowledge sharing programs to expand expertise and career opportunities.

Healthcare

We provide additional private health coverage with an included annual full medical examination, extra premiums for dental and ophthalmological care.

Sport

We offer an array of membership-based sport activities and encourage and sponsor our team members to participate in charitable runs or marathons.

Team Building

Encouraging collaboration, fun and unity we organize frequent team building events all around the globe, while we also champion in team activities that support a positive and friendly work environment.

Stock Options

We like our team members to be more than employees and be a part of the company - as contributors, shareholders - and encourage their entrepreneur spirit by granting an annually based amount of company stocks.

Locations
